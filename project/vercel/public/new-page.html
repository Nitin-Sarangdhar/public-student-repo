Rules and Status HeartSeven

Rules

  • There are four players in this game who play clockwise starting with player 1.
  • A player passes if he does not have any valid card to play.
  • The first valid card is heart 7. Subsequent valid cards are 7 from any other suit or a card that is adjacent to an existing table card.
  • The player with no card left wins the game.
  • The remaining players get a penalty based on the remaining cards in their hands.
  • The game is played seven times to determine the winner.

Status

  • Current status of the game: Implemented with four automatic players.
